四级做题技巧 🔗

听力 🔗

  • 读选项–勾关键词，预测发音、意思，根据关键词与推测题意
  • 绝对化选项注意可能是错误的
  • but,however转折后可能跟答案
  • 一句话出现两个选项则两个选项都不选

仔细阅读 🔗

  • 先看题目——推测文章大意——定位出题句——对比选项——找出答案
  • 顺序原则
  • total–entire–whole–complete–all出现这些词同义替换基本确定答案

长篇阅读 🔗

  • 读标题

  • 找并划出选项关键词，5个为一组，一组找完念两遍

  • 选项与文章信息匹配：2+1（同义替换）原则

  • 匹配超过30秒先下一题

翻译 🔗

  1. 做题顺序 –找主干（谁是什么，谁做什么，什么被做）
  2. 添枝加叶（添加修饰成分）
  3. 检查回读（看看有没有漏翻的）
  4. 一个主干是一句话
  5. 了：完成时标志 has/have done

  • 的：of—–>A of B = B的A

  • 有：(1) have/has Tea has a history of 5000 years

    ​ (2) There be(there is/are/was/were)/has/have been There is a history of 5000 years of tea

    ​ (3)伴随的一种状态：有了这辆车，我上班再也不迟到了 With this car,i will no longer be late for work

选词填空 🔗

  • adj+n
  • n+adj
  • of+n
  • a/the+n

作文 🔗

议论文（标志词语：essay,wthat,thy）

模板 🔗

（开头）

​ （现象解释）With the rapid development of —–(society,culture,medical service,education,economy),it is great necessity for—-(students,residents,citizens,youngsters) to—-.It is quite natural for us to ponder over the question of—-(填主题词).

​ (观点选择) Nowadays,youngsters have different options upon ().some think that () is beneficial. while others think () is better choice. personally,I prefer the former/latter. the following reason can account for my preference.

（万能段）

（论点1）

​ first and foremost，there is no doubt that—-.

（论据1）

​ Based on big data,a majority of (succesful social elites) admitted that they have spent 2/3 of their (time) in (doing sth).

(论点2)

​ Furthermore(其次), it is obvious that—-.

(论据2)

​ where there is/are—-,there is/are —-.（有…就有…）

(论点3)

​ Last but not least,no one can deny that—-.

(论据3)

​ Although—-,but—-.(尽管…但是…)

(结尾)

​ In a word,the whole society should pay close attention to the problem of —-.Only in this way can we have a bright and promising future.

应用文

Dear sb(顶格写)
